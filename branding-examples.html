Branded Soap Bars

Many of these soap makers did not have had a company name, artwork, or a logo design when they contacted us. However, they had an idea.

Started by deciding the type of soap bar, then the shape of their soap bar, and finally, wanting the right weight for their soap bar (i.e., 90-grams; 3-ounces).

Next is the question of having enough area for their company name, logo, or artwork.

Next is the question to use engraved lettering or embossed lettering or a combination of both.

Next is the question of adding border to protect the embossed lettering.

And other options include adjusting the thickness of the soap bar. Decreasing the thickness can allow an increase of the surface area without adding weight.
